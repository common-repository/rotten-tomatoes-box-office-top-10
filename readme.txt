Display the Rotten Tomatoes Box Office List on your blog.

== Description ==

Add official Tomatometer and Flixster scores to your blog with the Box Office Top 10 widget by Flixster. Follow the links to get official movie information from Rotten Tomatoes -- the most beloved source for info and critic reviews


== Installation ==

###Uploading The Plugin###

Extract all files from the ZIP file, *do not change or move anything in the folder*, and then upload it to `/wp-content/plugins/`.

**See Also:** ["Installing Plugins" article on the WP Codex](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Installing_Plugins)

###Plugin Activation###

Go to the admin area of your WordPress install and click on the "Plugins" menu. Click on "Activate" for the "Rotten Tomatoes Box Office Top 10" plugin.

###Using the Plugin###

Click on Widgets under Apperance, drag the "RT Box Office" tab to the right pane. Configure the country and other options as needed.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What if I want the box office list for another country? =

Go to the Widgets menu item under Apperance and expand the "RT Box Office" tab. The default country is US, but we also support United Kingdom and Australia.
